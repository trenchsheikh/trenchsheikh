## 🧱 Selected Work

**[Cloak](https://github.com/trenchsheikh/cloak)** &nbsp;·&nbsp; Privacy trading infrastructure on Solana
> Stealth buys, stealth exits, private limit orders, and DCA — all routed through executor wallets so your main wallet never touches the DEX. Built on Jupiter with TweetNaCl encryption and a non-custodial escrow program in Rust.
> `Next.js` `Solana` `Rust` `Jupiter` `TypeScript`

---

**[AsterDex Trading Bot](https://github.com/trenchsheikh/asterdex-trading-bot)** &nbsp;·&nbsp; Telegram-native perps trading bot
> Multi-user perps trading bot for Aster Finance. Handles wallet provisioning, dynamic leverage detection across 100+ symbols, real-time position management, and account ops — all via Telegram buttons. Projects built with this infrastructure have hit $1M+ trading volume.
> `Node.js` `Telegram Bot API` `BEP-20` `Perps` `JavaScript`

---

**[Xportal](https://github.com/trenchsheikh/Xportal-dashboard)** &nbsp;·&nbsp; AI agent-powered prediction market on Circle Arc
> Deploy personal AI micro-traders (Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Aggregator) that scan, score, and execute USDC trades autonomously on Polymarket. 220+ commits. Real-time dashboard with portfolio tracking, agent performance, and live market data.
> `Next.js` `TypeScript` `Circle Arc` `USDC` `Polymarket` `Zustand` `TanStack`

---

**[UnderWriter](https://github.com/trenchsheikh/underwriter-cursorhack)** &nbsp;·&nbsp; Autonomous VC diligence agent
> Six concurrent diligence desks fan out in parallel, stream findings live, and surface a verdict in 60 seconds. Catches BEC fraud. When blocked, a Cursor Composer 2 agent drafts a mandate amendment as a GitHub PR — the fund's policy compounds in git.
> `Next.js` `TypeScript` `OpenAI` `Cursor SDK` `SSE` `Specter` `MCP`

---

**[LobSmash](https://github.com/trenchsheikh/lobsmash)** &nbsp;·&nbsp; League platform for padel
> Full league management — shareable join codes, role-based access, format-aware leaderboards (Summit, Americano, Round Robin, Mexicano), and a portable Elo-style global skill rating that follows players across every league they play in. Live at [app.lobsmash.com](https://app.lobsmash.com).
> `Next.js` `TypeScript` `Tailwind` `Framer Motion` `Vercel`
